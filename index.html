About Us

TOPGRILL is the new place in town. A combination of one of the top car-care brands in the country - EARL's, and the new place to hangout with friends and family - TOPGRILL.
Located in Dau, Mabalacat City, Pampanga, it offers the best spa for your car during the day and the best dishes and beers at night.
